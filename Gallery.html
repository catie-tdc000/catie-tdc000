Prison Art and the Cycle.

"In shadows deep, a soul confined,
Yet in those walls, a light did find,
With every stroke of ink and hue,
A canvas born from pain, anew.
Through bars of iron, dreams took flight,
Each drawing spoke of hidden light,\
A jagged heart transformed to art,
In solitude, I found my heart.
Lines of struggle, shades of hope,
In every piece, I learned to cope,
From darkness came a vibrant flare,
My spirit soared, embraced the air.
Each masterpiece, a tale untold,
Of freedom forged in silent bold,
So here I stand, no longer bound,
In colors bright, my strength I've found."
